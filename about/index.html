About

I am an engineer by degree, and a cost planner by trade. I have a wife, a boston terrier and very soon a child. I like to consider myself an early adopter, yet can also be very reluctant to change. I have always been facinated by computers and in recent days have found myself becoming a proponent of open source software. I use Windows at work, Linux at home on an old MacBook. I love building things and consider myself to be a competant wood-worker and all around DIY-er. In general I'd say I'm a jack of all trades. The latest trade I find myself interest in learning more about is python programming and web development, which should be a great fit as I think it would be difficult to master this topic.

As I attempt to write on this blog, I would think that the topics might cover a wide spectrum. Thoughts on technology, software, DIY projects, child-rearing, religion, music, entertainment... could all be topics of conversation. If for no other reason, this will be a record of on man's thoughts on life.
